MiniSpecialFunctions - Package Overview
=======================================

The mini-special-functions package provides Lean 4 definitions and
theorems for classical special functions of complex analysis.

Core Functions
--------------
1. ComplexExponential (e^z)        - Entire, periodic 2*pi*i
2. ComplexTrig (sin z, cos z, tan z) - Via Euler formulas
3. ComplexLogarithm (principal branch) - Multivalued, branch cut (-inf,0]
4. ComplexPower (z^alpha)          - z^alpha = exp(alpha * log z)
5. GammaFunction (Gamma(z))        - Meromorphic, poles at 0,-1,-2,...
6. RiemannZeta (zeta(s))           - Meromorphic, simple pole at s=1
7. WeierstrassP (wp(z; Lambda))    - Doubly periodic elliptic
8. JacobiTheta (theta(z; tau))     - Quasi-doubly periodic
9. ModularForms                     - f(M*tau) = (c*tau+d)^k f(tau)

Core Theorems
-------------
- Euler reflection: Gamma(z)*Gamma(1-z) = pi/sin(pi*z)
- Legendre duplication: Gamma(z)*Gamma(z+1/2) = 2^(1-2z)*sqrt(pi)*Gamma(2z)
- Zeta functional equation: zeta(s) = 2^s*pi^(s-1)*sin(pi*s/2)*Gamma(1-s)*zeta(1-s)
- Jacobi triple product identity
- Weierstrass PDE: (wp')^2 = 4*wp^3 - g2*wp - g3
- Modularity of theta functions
- Classification of elliptic functions
- Modular forms graded ring = C[E4, E6]
- Bohr-Mollerup: Gamma is unique log-convex factorial extension
